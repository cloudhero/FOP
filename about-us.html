FOP Development Group (Exclusive US Distributor)

Our parther FOP Development Group (FOP). is the exclusive distributor in the U.S. FOP provides comprehensive environmental solutions by integrating the disciplines of project management, the art of strategic planning and the power of advanced technology. FOP offers a variety of professional environmental services, technology and wastewater financing approaches. FOP's programs offer valuable LEED points, as well as water-energy conservation and sustainability applications. Our experts and industry partners have distinguished careers as leaders in environmental applications throughout the world. FOP delivers top quality technology and techniques to meet the tough "Green" challenges posed by today's regulatory agencies.


Thomas Brothers Hydro (Design and Engineering)

Our partner, Thomas Brothers Hydro assisted in the design, engineering and manufacturing of the VFH Turbine™ process. Two brothers, one an electrical engineer and the other a mechanical engineer founded the company. Both of them had brought their experience and knowledge to the company, helping it grow to where they are today. Over the years they have established themselves as a supplier of parts and service in Canada, the US, Central and South America. Clientele include dozens of major power companies, US Army Corps, US Department of Defense, numerous municipalities, various manufacturing facilities, GSA and individually owned and operated hydropower generating stations. From manufacturing to design, their highly trained professionals have been handpicked from among the best in the industry and are trained to provide you with the solutions you need – as well as the quality you deserve.



West Point Industries (Fabrication and Assembly)

Our partner, West Point Industries (WPI) a Georgia-based company, will fabricate, manufacture and assemble the VFH Turbine™ system. Founded in 1868, WPI has a very impressive history and track record of quality workmanship and performance. With an 188,000 sq. ft foundry, WPI has all the capabilities to manufacture, assemble, test, warehouse and ship the VFH system.

They will source and inventory components that are not being fabricated onsite. Their experts will assemble the entire turnkey system. Assembly services set West Point apart from all other Contract Manufacturers. They have over 100 years in assembling their own patented line of engineered products that have been sold internationally under the "West Point" name.

Their experienced mechanics and electricians utilize the same skill, quality and attention to detail in contract assembly services. They have expertise in all types of mechanical assemblies, including pneumatic and hydraulic expertise. WPI electricians are capable of wiring all the switches and controls for VFH assemblies, building Process Control Panels, and wiring for all types of drive systems.



P.F. Moon and Company (Civil Engineering)

Our partner P.F. Moon and Company's project management and construction team is our exclusive Civil Enginerring installer. They have over 30 years of specialized experience in projects that improve water supply, wastewater treatment and industrial waste pretreatment facilities. Their projects have ranged in size from a small industrial pre-treatment facility to a multi-million gallon per day water treatment plant.

With over 1,000 projects in their portfolio, they are the ideal installation partner for the VFH Turbine™ installations. They have developed a comprehensive pre-installation plan as well as the final installation and operation of each turbine.

P.F. Moon and Company has earned the confidence and trust of clients such as Frito-Lay and Waste Management, US Army Corp of Engineers, and Gwinnett County, Georgia by providing innovative and cost effective construction solutions. We are proud to have them on our team.
